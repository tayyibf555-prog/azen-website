The Problem.

The practice needed a digital presence that matched their energetic and playful real-world environment, but they were stuck with an outdated, rigid layout. More critically, they had no centralized infrastructure to capture prospective parent leads, track inquiry statuses, or manage the enrollment pipeline over time.

CRM & Frontend Design.

We designed a vibrant, highly engaging web interface that appeals directly to modern parents while strictly maintaining institutional trust. Behind the aesthetic frontend, we wired a custom CRM system to autonomously aggregate inquiries, track prospective students, and streamline their entire enrollment pipeline.
